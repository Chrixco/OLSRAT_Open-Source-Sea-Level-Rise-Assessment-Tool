  <h2>üåç OSLRAT - Open-Source Sea-Level Rise Assessment Tool</h2>

  sea-level rise and extreme water level scenarios with cutting-edge climate science integration.
  </p>
  <p>
  <b>üî¨ Climate Science Integration:</b>
  <ul>
    <li><b>IPCC AR6 (2021)</b> global mean sea-level projections (5 SSP scenarios)</li>
    <li><b>FACTS framework (2023)</b> for parametric & structural uncertainty</li>
    <li><b>CODEC return period datasets</b> for storm surge & tide extremes (1-1000 year)</li>
    <li><b>OpenTopography DEMs</b> (Copernicus 30m/90m) and user-provided elevation data</li>
  </ul>
  </p>
  <p>
  <b>üìä New Features in v0.2:</b>
  <ul>
    <li><b>Interactive Data Visualization Dashboard</b> - Charts, statistics, and comparisons</li>
    <li><b>OSM Data Fetching</b> - Download buildings and streets via Overpass API</li>
    <li><b>Population Impact Analysis</b> - Proportional distribution and filtering</li>
    <li><b>Memory & Performance Improvements</b> - Fixed leaks, optimized dialogs</li>
    <li><b>Enhanced User Experience</b> - Non-modal windows, better workflow</li>
  </ul>
  </p>
  <p>
  Designed for <i>scientists, urban planners, emergency managers, and decision-makers</i>,
  the plugin supports:
  <ul>
    <li>Parcel/building-level flood risk evaluation</li>
    <li>Social vulnerability index (SVI) calculation</li>
    <li>Interactive visualization with export capabilities</li>
    <li>Evidence-based planning for <b>climate adaptation</b> and <b>resilient cities</b></li>
  </ul>
  </p>
